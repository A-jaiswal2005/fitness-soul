Yoga for Back Pain & Spine Health

These carefully selected asanas help strengthen the back muscles, improve spinal flexibility, and relieve tension in the vertebral column. Practice regularly for lasting relief.

Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose)

Duration: 30-60 seconds (3-5 rounds)
Best Time: Morning on empty stomach
Frequency: Daily
Therapeutic Benefits:
  • Strengthens the spine
  • Stretches chest and shoulders
  • Improves flexibility of upper and middle back
  • Relieves mild backache

Balasana (Child's Pose)

Duration: 1-3 minutes
Best Time: Anytime (especially after backbends)
Frequency: Daily, especially when experiencing pain
Therapeutic Benefits:
  • Gently stretches lower back
  • Relieves spinal compression
  • Calms the nervous system
  • Reduces stress and fatigue

Ardha Matsyendrasana (Half Lord of the Fishes Pose)

Duration: 30-60 seconds per side
Best Time: Morning or evening (3+ hours after meals)
Frequency: 3-5 times per week
Therapeutic Benefits:
  • Increases spinal flexibility
  • Relieves stiffness in back muscles
  • Stimulates spinal nerves
  • Improves posture and alignment

Yoga for Stress & Anxiety Relief

These calming asanas and practices help activate the parasympathetic nervous system, reduce cortisol levels, and promote deep relaxation.

Viparita Karani (Legs-Up-the-Wall Pose)

Duration: 5-15 minutes
Best Time: Evening before dinner or before bed
Frequency: Daily during stressful periods
Therapeutic Benefits:
  • Calms the nervous system
  • Reduces anxiety and mild depression
  • Relieves tired legs and feet
  • Helps lower blood pressure

Savasana (Corpse Pose)

Duration: 10-20 minutes
Best Time: Anytime, especially after practice
Frequency: Daily (essential after yoga practice)
Therapeutic Benefits:
  • Deep relaxation for body and mind
  • Reduces stress, anxiety and tension
  • Lowers blood pressure
  • Helps relieve insomnia

Sukhasana (Easy Pose) with Pranayama

Duration: 5-10 minutes (with Nadi Shodhana)
Best Time: Early morning or evening
Frequency: Twice daily during stressful periods
Therapeutic Benefits:
  • Calms the mind and reduces anxiety
  • Balances left and right brain hemispheres
  • Improves focus and mental clarity
  • Reduces stress hormone levels
